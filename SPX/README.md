📈 S&P 500 Historical Analysis Dashboard

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard created to analyze the historical performance of the S&P 500 Index. The goal was to transform raw stock market data into meaningful insights through clear visualizations and an easy-to-use interface.

The dashboard allows users to explore market trends, trading activity, yearly performance, and return patterns over different time periods. With interactive filters and dynamic visuals, users can quickly identify important market movements and gain a better understanding of historical S&P 500 performance.

📌 About the Project

The S&P 500 is one of the most widely followed stock market indices, making it an excellent dataset for financial analysis. In this dashboard, historical market data was analyzed to uncover trends in prices, trading volume, and returns over time.

The dashboard is designed using a modern dark theme and focuses on providing a clean and professional user experience while highlighting key financial metrics.

🚀 Dashboard Highlights
📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

At the top of the dashboard, important market metrics are displayed, including:

Latest Closing Price
Highest Recorded Price
Lowest Recorded Price
Average Trading Volume

These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of market performance.

📈 Trend Analysis

The dashboard includes visualizations that track market behavior over time:

Year-End Close by Year to understand long-term market growth.
Trading Volume by Year to analyze changes in market activity.
📉 Return Analysis

To evaluate market performance from different perspectives, the dashboard provides:

Average Monthly Return Percentage
Yearly Performance Percentage

These visuals help identify periods of strong growth and market downturns.

🔍 Price and Volume Relationship

A scatter plot is used to examine the relationship between trading volume and closing prices, helping users understand how market activity changes with price movements.

🎛 Interactive Filters

Users can customize their analysis using:

Date Range Filter
Year Filter
Month Filter
Volume Range Filter
Close Price Range Filter
Reset Filters Button

These filters make the dashboard highly interactive and user-friendly.
